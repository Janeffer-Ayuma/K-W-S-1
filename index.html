The Big 5 & More

African Lion

King of the savanna, known for its majestic mane and powerful roar.

Did you know? A lion's roar can be heard from as far as 8 kilometers (5 miles) away!

African Elephant

The largest living land animal, recognized by its long trunk and large ears.

Did you know? An elephant's trunk contains over 40,000 muscles, but no bones.

African Leopard

A solitary and nocturnal cat, famous for its spotted coat and incredible stealth.

Did you know? Leopards are excellent climbers and often haul their prey into trees.

Maasai Giraffe

The tallest land animal, with unique coat patterns similar to human fingerprints.

Did you know? Giraffes only need to drink water once every few days.

Plains Zebra

Known for its distinctive black and white stripes, each pattern is unique.

Did you know? A group of zebras is called a "dazzle."

Cheetah

The world's fastest land animal, capable of reaching speeds of 120 km/h.

Did you know? Cheetahs can accelerate from 0 to 96 km/h (60 mph) in just three seconds.
